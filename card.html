Cat

A cat can’t climb head first down a tree because every claw on a cat’s paw points the same way. To get down from a tree, a cat must back down.

  • Scientific Name: Felis silvestris catus
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Food: Small meats
  • Habitats: Forests, coastal areas, urban areas, and etc

Cats have pointy ears, round eyes,little claws,and short but soft fur.Cats like eating fish and mice.They like to keep themselves clean, and they do that by licking their fur.Also they are nocturnal animals, they sleep in the daytime and do most of their movements at night.
